Project-186 is a human-focused project aiming for an ambitious future. We are not a corporation or a centralized organization. Project-186 is an idea: a symbol of revolution.
To join Project-186, you can enter your email here, or contact project-186@proton.me with specific requests. More generally, contribute to the Project by studying its vision, exploring its resources, and spreading the word.
Project-186 is always growing. In general, the most important goal of the Project is to spread itself, to solidify itself as a meme, and to recognize a shared vision for the future. More specific goals can be found on our goals page.
Problems such as social isolation, a lack of fulfillment, or dependencies on technology are common in modern settings, almost paradoxically: shouldn't progress exist in order to relieve suffering, rather than create it? Even if you do not personally struggle, you likely have family or friends who do. Through Project-186, societal progress will be reoriented in order to improve the lives of all.
Project-186 defines itself as a natural part of humanity’s evolution. Fundamentally, the Project believes that individuals must hold themselves accountable for their actions and embrace the future in order for humanity to progress. The philosophy of Project-186 can be understood further through the pillars and resources.

Pillars

Project-186 believes that human potential must be protected and valued. The pursuit of potential, is precisely and exactly how society improves in order to. Everybody’s potential is different, but potential is infinite: there are always ways to improve.
Project-186 knows that new technologies must be accepted as being a part of the world. The Project criticizes certain uses of technology, but we do not attempt to stop progress; as we understand that this is impossible. The goal of Project-186 is alignment of future progress, not the destruction of past progress.
Project-186 is not tied to a central authority: the Project is what it is. Anybody can participate, however, the Project is layered on top of several existing structures, which aim to provide a core philosophical and practical baseline for the endeavors of the Project. Certain centralized elements are necessary to represent the Project (websites, emails addresses, etc.) but by definition, there is no authority that decides what is “official” or not.
Advertisement is understood by the Project to a corruption of the natural lifecycle of memes. Some level of advertisement is necessary; for example, if the Project spreads by promoting itself in youtube comments, or physical fliers, than this is a form of advertisement. But the Project understands that advocating for ideas should be voluntary and willing, reflecting human incentives, not financial incentives.
Project-186 is optimistic about the future. There is no particular reason that optimism is held, but it is a necessary part of building a better future. The Project looks at global trends of human suffering, recognizing that on large scales, standard of living rises.
